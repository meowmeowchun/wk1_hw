About Cats

The Cat (Felis Catus) is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.

There are many colors within the cat species, including the Orange Cat and the Black Cat.

Features

Cats are not always completely stupid. Some can be very smart. They have very small, curved claws and cute paws.
